PhilHealth Online NHIP Registration - Individual

Instruction in Terms and Condition

Mabuhay! This Online Facility is designed to provide easy and convenient way of registration in the National Health Insurance Program (NHIP) and corresponding issuance of your PhilHealth Identification Number (PIN).

However, it is emphasized herein that duly accomplished PhilHealth Membership Registration Form (PMRF) and supporting document/s (e.g. birth certificate, valid, acceptable, or duly issued ID) is necessary and needs to be uploaded for the system to properly validate and process the application otherwise, it will be disregarded.

All electronically submitted registration forms with complete supporting document/s shall be processed within three (3) to five (5) working days. The PIN will be sent to the provided email address of the registrant upon successful processing of the registration.

Further, please be reminded that a valid email address is required and it can only be used once. A system-generated "Notification" will be sent regarding the status of the registration. It must be noted that a registrant cannot submit more than one (1) application.

In using the PhilHealth Electronic Registration System, please take note of the following:
  1. Fields marked with an asterisk (*) are mandatory. The system will not proceed unless all required fields are properly completed.
  2. Your registration application will undergo evaluation and approval by an assigned PhilHealth processor.
  3. Click the [Submit Registration] button to submit your application. A confirmation pop-up message will appear, informing you of the next steps to complete your registration.
  4. All submitted registration forms with the necessary attachments will be processed within three (3) to five (5) working days from the date of submission. Applications with incorrect or missing supporting documents will be automatically rejected.
  5. Your PhilHealth Identification Number (PIN) will be issued once your registration is successfully processed. This PIN is permanent and uniquely assigned to you.
I hereby agree to comply with the requirements stated above, including the submission of all necessary information and supporting documents. I further declare, under penalty of law, that all information I have provided in this online registration platform is true and accurate to the best of my knowledge.
